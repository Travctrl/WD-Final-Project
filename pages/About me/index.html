About me

Formerly a tourism student, I discovered my real path in IT after realizing how naturally curious I was about how technology works. Once I began learning programming, everything started to make sense, it felt like solving puzzles that I actually enjoyed. That moment of clarity made me shift paths and fully commit to exploring the world of code. Now, I’m eager to keep learning and growing in this field, turning curiosity into real skills that can build and solve.

My Hobbies

I’ve always loved playing sports because I enjoy staying physically active; it keeps me energized and engaged. While I try different kinds of sports, I mostly focus on basketball and badminton as my main games, where I can really challenge myself and have fun at the same time.

I also enjoy going to the gym. It helps clear my mind, relieves stress, and allows me to track my physical progress. It’s become a routine that keeps both my body and mindset in check.

Aside from fitness, I stream online where I love interacting with people in real time. It gives me a sense of connection, and sharing my interests through streaming has helped me build confidence and express myself more freely.
